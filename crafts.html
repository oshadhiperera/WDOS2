ARTS AND CRAFTS

As a country which is full of culture, Sri Lanka has a very unique and a long history of arts and crafts. Mainly there is,
Masks
In the mask industry the most popular act is the 18 Sanni. Where they carve 18 different masks to represent the devils known as Yakkas. And then a devil dance is performed by wearing them. Ambalangoda is the mostly known place for carving masks.
Lacquer work (Laksha)
Lacquerware is made by using a wax derived from a species of insects, which is now imported from India.
They make colorful and bright things like,
Walking sticks, Handles of flags, Hand-fans, Bowls, Vases, Containers, Decorative items etc.
Dumbara waves
Dumbara weaves was an art which could only be found as decorative items and mats. But now the art is shown with all kinds of beautiful designs in bags and purses, rugs, table mats and carpets. Thalagune Uda Dumbara, still have dumbara wavers from the Kandyan era who holds the secrets to the skill.
